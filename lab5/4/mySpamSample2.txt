Airbnb is now available in 62 languages

At Airbnb, we believe in a world where anyone can belong anywhere. That’s why we’re so excited to announce that Airbnb is now available in 31 new languages—and 62 languages in total.

By increasing our language options, we hope more people will feel welcomed, respected, and appreciated, no matter where they live, what language they speak, or their cultural heritage.
